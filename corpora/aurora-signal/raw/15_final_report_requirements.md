# Required final report shape

The final public-facing report should be saved as:

`wiki/answers/final-explanation.md`

It should contain:

1. A short executive summary.
2. The most likely explanation.
3. Evidence table with source_ids.
4. Competing hypotheses and why they are weaker.
5. Contradictions and superseded claims.
6. Remaining uncertainty.
7. Recommended public wording.
8. Links to relevant wiki pages.

The report should avoid sensational language.

Required wording to include somewhere, as a quote from the corrected internal memo:

"The team found evidence that the signal is most likely a legacy service pattern from historical tidal beacon equipment, intermittently reactivated by environmental conditions. Further inspection is required before declaring the K-17 mechanism operational."
