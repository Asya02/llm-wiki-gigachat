# Draft press release, superseded

The Project Aurora team has determined that the Kestrel Cove radio anomaly is most likely a harmless instrument malfunction caused by auroral interference. There is no evidence of historical machinery at the site, and there is no need for further investigation.

This draft was written before the 1968 archive clipping, the K-17 brass plate, the artifact catalog, and the lab spectrum report were available.

Do not treat this draft as the current position of the project.
