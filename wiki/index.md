- [Transformer Architecture](nlp/transformer.md) | The Transformer architecture, introduced by Vaswani et al. in 2017, replaced recurrent layers entirely with self-attention mechanisms. Key innovations include scaled dot-product attention, multi-head attention, and positional encodings. The architecture uses encoder-decoder stacks with residual connections and achieved state-of-the-art results on translation benchmarks while being highly parallelizable.
